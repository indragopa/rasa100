About the Celebrations

The Śatamāna RāSa programme spans the centenary year of Dr. R. Sathyanarayana's birth (9 May 2027), encompassing a series of concerts, lectures, seminars, publication releases and commemorative gatherings. Organised by Brahmavidya (R.), these events honour the totality of his contribution to Indian music, dance, Sanskrit scholarship and Indology.

All events are rooted in the spirit of the gurukula: simple, reverent, and dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge. The programme unfolds in phases throughout the year, with the central celebrations around the birth anniversary in May 2027.

Phase I: Pūrva Samarambha (Pre-Centenary)

January - April 2027
Time / Date Event Details
January 16, 2027
Smṛti Dina: Remembrance Day
Seventh anniversary of the mahāprasthāna
 Floral tributes, invocation and addresses by family, disciples and scholars. Screening of archival recordings. Held at Sādhana Dhāma, Mysuru.
February 2027
Vyākhyāna Mālā: Lecture Series
Eminent musicologists and Indologists
 A series of monthly lecture-demonstrations on the musicological contributions of Dr. Sathyanarayana, covering topics from śruti theory to compositional analysis.
March 2027
Grantha Lōkārpaṇa: Book Release
Centenary Commemorative Volume
 Release of the centenary commemorative publication containing scholarly articles, reminiscences, and reprints of important works by Dr. Sathyanarayana.
April 2027
Nṛtya Darśana: Dance Presentation
Bharatanāṭyam recital dedicated to his danceology
 A Bharatanāṭyam presentation exploring the danceological insights of Dr. Sathyanarayana, preceded by a lecture on his contributions to Nṛtya Śāstra.

Phase II: Janma Śatamānōtsava (Birth Centenary)

May 2027
Time / Date Event Details
May 9, 2027 (Morning)
Śubhārambha: Inaugural Ceremony
Chief Guest to be announced
 Ceremonial inauguration of the centenary celebrations with Vedic invocations, lighting of the lamp, garlanding of the portrait, and inaugural address. Felicitation of senior disciples.
May 9, 2027 (Afternoon)
Saṅgīta Sabhā: Concert
Vidwan Dr. R. S. Nandakumar and disciples
 A Karnataka classical music concert by the son and foremost disciple, Vidwan Dr. R. S. Nandakumar, featuring compositions from the guru paramparā of Sadguru Tyāgarāja.
May 9, 2027 (Evening)
Sabhā Gōṣṭhī: Symposium
Distinguished scholars and musicians
 Panel discussions on the significance of Dr. Sathyanarayana's musicological contributions, the state of Karnataka music scholarship today, and the future of the guru-śiṣya paramparā.
May 10, 2027
Vidvat Sabhā: Scholars' Assembly
National and international musicologists
 A day-long academic conference featuring paper presentations on Dr. Sathyanarayana's contributions to musicology, danceology and Indology, with participants from universities across India and abroad.
May 11, 2027
Saṅgīta Utsava: Music Festival
Karnataka Classical musicians
 A full-day music festival featuring senior and emerging Karnataka Classical musicians, with concerts held in the traditional intimate format of the gurukula.

Phase III: Varṣapūrṇa (Year-Long Activities)

June 2027 - May 2028
Time / Date Event Details
Monthly
Māsika Saṅgīta Sabhā
Rotating artists from the paramparā
 Monthly concerts in the gurukula tradition, featuring musicians from the lineage and invitees, held on the second Saturday of each month.
Quarterly
Vyākhyāna: Centenary Lectures
Invited scholars
 Quarterly public lectures on musicology, danceology and Indology, named in honour of Dr. R. Sathyanarayana, by eminent scholars.
Ongoing
Grantha Pramōcana: Publication Programme
Brahmavidya (R.)
 Phased release of new editions and reprints of Dr. Sathyanarayana's key works, including Puṇḍarīkamala, Śruti: The Scalic Foundation, and Karnataka Saṅgīta Vāhinī.
Ongoing
Archival Documentation Project
Brahmavidya (R.) team
 Digitisation and archiving of audio recordings, manuscripts, photographs and correspondence from Dr. Sathyanarayana's personal collection.

Participation and Venue

All events are open to scholars, students, musicians and members of the public. Venues will be announced closer to each event. The principal celebrations in May 2027 will be held in Mysuru, the city where Dr. Sathyanarayana lived and worked for most of his life.

For details, registration and updates, please visit the Contact page or write to Brahmavidya (R.).
